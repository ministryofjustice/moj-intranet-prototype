What you need to know

Work can be good for your physical and mental health. It plays an important part in your wellbeing.

There may be times when you can’t do your job properly or at all and require support.

The interactive MoJ Wellbeing Strategy and strategy annex: ‘Where to go for support’, outline MoJ’s commitment to your wellbeing.

There’s a range of support available to you.

Talk to your manager or look at the Employee Assistance Programme (EAP) leaflet pdf or visit the EAP pages for confidential advice and information.
