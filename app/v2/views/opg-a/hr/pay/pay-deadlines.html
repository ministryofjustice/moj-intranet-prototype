What you need to know

So you can get claims and change requests paid quickly, pay deadline dates have been set up.

You can make some changes in Phoenix, but mostly you need to send a form to Shared Services.

 

Way of submitting changes Deadline for inclusion in that month’s salary
Phoenix 6pm on date shown in pay deadlines document (submitted and approved by manager)
Form 8am on date shown in pay deadlines document (form must have reached Shared Services by this deadline)
