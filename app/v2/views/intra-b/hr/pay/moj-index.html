Pay and benefits

  • Content owner: MoJ HR
  • Last updated: 27 March 2016
How you're paid, the benefits on offer and how to claim overtime.
  • Tab 1

In this section you can find out more about being paid as an employee in the Ministry of Justice.  Shared Services work with the Liberata to process all payments in a timely manner.

Your pay will be paid into your bank account on the last working day of each month, any changes to your pay will be paid in the next available pay period (provided they are received before each pay cut-off date).

It is your responsibility to check your pay each month and highlight any issues accordingly.  If there are any inaccuracies in your pay please contact Shared Services immediately so that your pay can be corrected and overpayments reclaimed or underpayments made.
